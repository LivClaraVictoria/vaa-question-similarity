INTERPRETATION
====================================================================================================

Most parties' top-5 benefiting questions are spread across low-to-medium
correlation topics. Average topic mean|r| for top-5 ranges from 0.199 (Green)
to 0.290 (SVP), close to the overall mean of 0.238.

Key observations:
- Immigration (|r|=0.45) appears for Centre and FDP/SVP — strongest signal
- Centre's top 5 mostly in low-corr topics (1/5 high-corr) — CRW would not
  detect strategic addition of these questions via correlation
- SVP (3/5) and FDP (2/5) have more questions in high-corr topics
- CRW detects redundancy within clusters, not strategic topic loading
- Adding genuinely different questions from different topics won't trigger
  CRW regardless of distance metric quality
